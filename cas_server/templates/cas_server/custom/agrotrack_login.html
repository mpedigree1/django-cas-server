AgroTrack

Online Management System For Agro-inputs Traceability

The Kenya Plant Health Inspectorate Service (KEPHIS) is Kenya's lead regulator in the agricultural sector working to ensure that the highest, world class, standards are implemented and observed in Kenya's crops farming sector.

This Portal represents the next level of technological advancement in the delivery of KEPHIS' mandate. The Agency's goal is to ensure that agro-inputs within its regulatory purview, especially seeds, are certified in a rigorous, secure, transparent and effective manner. It has thus, in collaboration with its partners, implemented an AgroTrack solution based on smart-tags and mobile phone and web technologies to achieve full traceability of the inputs supply chain in the medium-term.
